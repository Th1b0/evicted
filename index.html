What does ANC stand for?
ANC stands for Active Noise Cancellation. It is a technology used in audio devices, including headphones and earphones, to reduce or eliminate ambient noise. With ANC, you can enjoy a more immersive and focused audio experience by minimizing external sounds and distractions.
